THEMATIC AREA

Education

Food for Life Nepal (FFLN) is a non-governmental, non-profit organization dedicated to the well-being of children in Nepal.

EDUCATION

Food For LifeNepal is dedicated to collaborating with governments to ensure that everyschool-aged child has access to nourishing meals, fostering health andreadiness for learning. In Budhanilkantha and Tokha Municipality, numerouschildren attend school on empty stomachs daily, impacting their concentrationand learning abilities. Additionally, many children, especially girls, are unable to attend school due to familial obligations in the fields or domestic tasks.

Since 2015 AD, Food For Life Nepal has been instrumentalin addressing these challenges through its Mid-day meal program. Thisinitiative serves as a transformative force across multiple sectors, enhancingchildren's education, health, and nutrition. HANDWASH, SANITATION & NUTRITION AWARENESS

At Food For Life Nepal, we go beyond just givingmeals; we focus on the overall well-being of the children in our Mid-Day Mealprogram. Through regular  campaigns, weeducate students with essential knowledge on proper handwashing, sanitationpractices, and the importance of maintaining a balanced and nutritious diet forhealthy wellbeing. Our initiatives include educating students about thepotential health risks associated with excessive consumption of processed andunhealthy foods, with a particular emphasis on avoiding junk food. FFLNholistic approach goes hand in hand with our mission to create a positive andlasting impact on the communities we serve.

COOKING PROCESS
CLEANSING

Our kitchen undergoes thorough washing and cleaning to eliminate any chance of contamination. Our cooks sanitize themselves before entering, and the staff wears protective gear. Equipped with state-of-the-art machinery made of food-grade stainless steel. Moreover, water is tested at regular intervals for various key parameters for purity. Last but not the least, all food ingredients are thoroughly washed and rinsed prior to cooking to maintain high hygiene standards.
